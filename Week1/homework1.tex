\item[(a)]Work out the ``algebra'' of the generators of 3D rotations $J_i$. \\
Where $
J_3 =  \begin{bmatrix}    0 & -i & 0  \\ i & 0  & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}, 
\hfill
 J_2 =  \begin{bmatrix}    0 & 0 & -i  \\ 0 & 0  & 0 \\ i & 0 & 0 \end{bmatrix}

\end{itemize}
